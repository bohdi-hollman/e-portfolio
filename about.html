About

Greetings! My name is Bohdi Hollman, and I am a third-year student at the University of Montana. I am currently majoring in Computer Science, with a concentration in Algorthm Development, while double-minoring in Philosophy and Ethics. In addition to this, I am an Honors student and Ambassador of the Davidson Honors College here at the university. I also have a peculiar interest in Biblical and theological studies, which consumes much of my free time. I quickly began to notice that there was a great need for someone to become an intermediary between the laypeople of the Church and the rich content of the intellectual powerhouses of theology. In an effort to teach everyone this advanced, academic theology in an accessible, easy-to-understand manner, I founded Coram Cruce Ministries in early 2022. This ministry, which interacts with both high scholarship and layperson alike, extends through various platforms and media to make learning the deep things of the Bible to be much less intimidating. Outside of all of these endeavours, you could find me on a long-distance run, sketching something with a micron pen, studying Thomas Jefferson, learning a new language, reading, or serving Resonate Church Missoula.
